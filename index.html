Our Story

Every great love story has a beginning, and ours started when two paths crossed at just the right moment. What began as a chance encounter grew into something beautiful and lasting.

Through shared laughter, quiet moments, and countless adventures both big and small, we discovered that we were meant to walk through life together. We found in each other not just love, but friendship, understanding, and a shared vision for the future.

Our journey together has been filled with joy, growth, and the kind of love that makes everything brighter. As we take this next step in our story, we're excited to celebrate with the people who matter most to us.

Thank you for being part of our lives and for joining us as we begin this new chapter together. We can't wait to share this special day with you and create new memories that will last a lifetime.

Wedding Registry

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift we could ask for! However, if you would like to honor us with a gift, we would be grateful for contributions toward our future together.

Our Preference: Cash Gifts

As we begin this new chapter of our lives, we're excited to build our home and future together. Rather than traditional registry items, we would be incredibly grateful for monetary gifts that will help us:

  • Save for our dream home
  • Plan our honeymoon adventure
  • Invest in experiences and memories together

How to Give

If you would like to contribute, here are a few convenient options:

  • Wedding Day: Cash or check gifts can be brought to the reception
  • Digital Payments: We accept Apple Pay, PayPal, Zelle or other digital payment methods

Please know that your love and support mean more to us than any gift. We're simply grateful to have you in our lives and can't wait to celebrate with you!
